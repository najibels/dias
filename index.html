Real Impact

A Tale of Two Investments

See how our platform transforms the investment experience for the Somali diaspora.

The Traditional Way

Ali, a business owner in Toronto, wired $30,000 to an "uncle" starting a shipping business in Somalia, promised a 50% return. After initial confirmation, communication stopped.

Ali's relatives discovered the truth: the money bought a house in Hargeisa. No business existed. Ali lost everything with no documentation or recourse.

The Better Way

Through our platform, Ali invested the same $30,000 across verified opportunities: shipping logistics, solar energy, and agriculture. All projects were professionally vetted with legal protections and regular reporting.

After three years, his investment grew to $46,500 with quarterly reports showing 47 jobs created. The shipping company expanded to three cities, the solar project powered 350 homes, and farmers increased their income.

Ali built wealth while creating sustainable change in Somalia—without blind trust in informal connections.

Remittances Reimagined

From consumption to sustainable investment with small monthly contributions

The Traditional Way

Amina, a nurse in Minneapolis, sends $300 monthly to her family in Mogadishu. Over five years, she's sent $18,000—money that helped with immediate needs but quickly disappeared without creating lasting change or returns.

The Better Way

Amina now sends $200 for immediate family needs and invests $100 monthly in a solar project in her family's neighborhood. After 5 years, her $6,000 investment has grown to $8,200 and generates $1,230 annual income.

Beyond financial returns, her investment powers her family's home, created local jobs, and inspired five friends to invest. Amina supports her family today while building wealth and developing Somalia's future.
